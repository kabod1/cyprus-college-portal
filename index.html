Our Institutions

Three Colleges, One Standard of Excellence

Each college specialises in its field while sharing a unified commitment to academic rigour, industry relevance, and student success.

Ledra College

Educating since 2007 — Cyprus's premier professional studies college offering CYQAA-accredited degrees and diplomas. Institutional partner of the School of Automotive Engineering (SAE).

  • BA Business Administration
  • BSc Computer Science & IT
  • LLB Law (English)
  • BA Tourism & Hospitality Management
  • MA International Relations
  • MBA General Management

School of Culinary Arts

A world-class culinary institution offering professional training from foundational techniques to advanced gastronomy, pastry, and hospitality management.

  • BSc Culinary Arts & Gastronomy
  • Diploma in Pastry & Baking Arts
  • BA Food & Beverage Management
  • BSc Nutrition & Dietetics
  • Certificate in Mediterranean Cuisine
  • MSc Food Science & Technology
View All Programs

School of Automotive Engineering (SAE)

Cyprus's leading automotive training institution offering CYQAA-accredited diplomas and City & Guilds qualifications. Campuses in Nicosia (Dali) and Limassol. HRDA-funded programs available.

  • Diploma in Automotive Engineering (120 ECTS · 2 yrs)
  • City & Guilds — Level 2: Vehicle Maintenance
  • City & Guilds — Level 3: Light Vehicle Repair
  • City & Guilds — Level 4: Advanced Diagnostics
  • Technical Seminars & Professional Upskilling
  • EV & Hybrid Systems (Short Courses)
Accreditations: CYQAA/DIPAE · City & Guilds · HRDA Approved

How to Apply

Your Journey Starts Here

A simple, guided application process designed to get you started quickly and confidently.

1

Choose Your Program

Browse our catalog of 40+ programs across all three colleges and find the one that matches your passion and career goals.

2

Submit Application

Complete the online application form with your personal details, academic background, and supporting documents.

3

Review & Interview

Our admissions team reviews your application. Selected candidates are invited for an interview or placement assessment.

4

Enroll & Begin

Receive your acceptance letter, complete enrollment, pay fees, and prepare to join our vibrant campus community.
