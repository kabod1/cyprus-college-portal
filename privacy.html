2. Data We Collect

Account & Identity Data

  • Full name, student/staff ID number
  • Email address and institutional login credentials (stored hashed)
  • Date of birth, nationality, and passport/ID number (for enrollment)
  • Emergency contact details

Academic Data

  • Course enrollments, grades, GPA, and academic transcripts
  • Attendance records and timetable data
  • Assignment submissions and academic correspondence

Financial Data

  • Tuition fee records, payment history, and outstanding balances
  • Financial aid applications and scholarship records

Technical Data

  • Authentication tokens stored in your browser's local storage
  • Session activity logs (login time, pages accessed)
  • Device type and browser information (for security purposes)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Delivering education services — managing your enrollment, grades, timetable, and academic records
  • Portal authentication — verifying your identity and maintaining secure session access
  • Financial administration — processing tuition fees, invoices, and financial aid
  • Communications — sending academic announcements, grade notifications, and institutional updates
  • Legal compliance — meeting obligations under Cypriot education law and CYQAA accreditation requirements
  • Safety & security — detecting and preventing unauthorised access to portal systems

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the following legal grounds under GDPR Article 6:

  • Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) — processing necessary to deliver education services under your enrollment agreement
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)) — compliance with CYQAA accreditation, Ministry of Education requirements, and Cypriot tax/financial law
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — portal security, fraud prevention, and institutional administration
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — optional cookies and non-essential analytics (where applicable)

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Railway.app — our backend API infrastructure provider (data processed within the EU/EEA)
  • CYQAA / Ministry of Education — as required by Cypriot accreditation law
  • University of Sunderland — for jointly-validated degree programs, where you are enrolled in such a program
  • City & Guilds London — for externally-certified qualification records
  • HRDA (Human Resource Development Authority) — for approved funded programs

All third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements and operate under GDPR-equivalent protections.

6. Data Retention

  • Student academic records: Retained for a minimum of 50 years following graduation (as required by CYQAA standards)
  • Financial records: 7 years following the end of the academic relationship (Cypriot tax law)
  • Authentication tokens: Expire after 24 hours; refresh tokens expire after 30 days
  • Application & enquiry data: 2 years from date of application
  • System access logs: 12 months for security purposes

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights:

Right of Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you (Subject Access Request).

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data where there is no overriding legal obligation to retain it.

Right to Restrict

Request that we limit processing of your data in certain circumstances.

Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests, including profiling.

To exercise any right, email dpo@ledra.ac.cy. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Cyprus Commissioner for Personal Data Protection at dataprotection.gov.cy.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

This portal uses local storage (not cookies) to store authentication tokens required for you to remain logged in. These are strictly necessary for the portal to function and do not require separate consent.

We use the following local storage keys:

  • cp_access_token — your session authentication token (expires 24h)
  • cp_refresh_token — token refresh credential (expires 30 days)
  • cp_user — your basic profile data (name, role) for display purposes
  • ccg_cookie_consent — your cookie consent preference

No third-party tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics scripts are loaded on this portal.

9. Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted via TLS 1.3 (HTTPS)
  • Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing (bcrypt)
  • Authentication uses short-lived JWT tokens with refresh rotation
  • The portal infrastructure is ISO 9001:2015 certified
  • Access to personal data is role-based and logged

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify the Cyprus DPA within 72 hours and affected individuals without undue delay.
